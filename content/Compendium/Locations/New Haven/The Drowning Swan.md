# The Drowning Swan
[[Kobe]] is the owner. [[Tika]] is the room cleaner and server. Bottom floor restaurant with an attached bar. Hostel style space outside under a canvas tent. Bring your own tent/sleeping bag. Five upstairs private rooms. You are responsible for securing your door.
